TWILIO SETUP GUIDE User Manual GUIDE ▼
1
Create a Twilio Account ▼

Go to twilio.com and click Sign Up.

Verify your email address and phone number when prompted.

After signup, you will land on the Twilio Console dashboard.

2
Get Your Credentials ▼

On the Console homepage, locate Account Info.

Account SID ACxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxxx
Auth Token your_auth_token_here

Paste these directly into the Call Alert Settings fields above.

3
WhatsApp Sandbox Setup ▼

In Twilio Console, go to Messaging → Try it Out → WhatsApp Sandbox.

You will see a join code like: join <word>

Open WhatsApp on your phone and send that exact join code to:

Sandbox Number whatsapp:+14155238886

Once joined, set WhatsApp From to whatsapp:+14155238886 in Notification Settings above.

4
Setup Voice Calling ▼

In Twilio Console, go to Phone Numbers → Manage → Buy a number.

Search for a number with Voice capability enabled. Trial accounts get $15 credit.

Once purchased, copy the number (e.g. +12015551234) and paste it into Twilio From Number in Call Alert Settings.

Calls are triggered automatically when: weapon detected, masked person detected, or confidence > 90%.

5
Connect with OmniEye ▼

Fill in all fields in the Call Alert Settings and Notification Settings sections above.

□ Phone Number — your personal number (receiver)
□ Twilio Account SID — from Console
□ Twilio Auth Token — from Console
□ Twilio From Number — your purchased Twilio number
□ Notification Number — your WhatsApp number
□ WhatsApp From — whatsapp:+14155238886

Click SAVE CONFIG when done.

6
Test the Integration ▼

After saving config, click TEST ALERT.

Expected results within 30 seconds:

✓ WhatsApp message received on your phone
✓ Voice call received (if call enabled)
✓ Alert appears in dashboard feed

If no alert arrives, verify that you have joined the WhatsApp sandbox and all credentials are correct.
